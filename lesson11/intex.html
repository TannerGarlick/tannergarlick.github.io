Welcome to OCS of Idaho

By Tanner Garlick


Here at the Oncoming Storm of Idaho we are creating a community where you will be prepared and ready to have the time of your life. We highlight three communities that include Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. We have the weather for each town and a five-day forecast so you can easily know if the weather will be right for your activities. We also have a Storm Center where you can report storms and let others know what is going on. We have a Gallery where community members can submit their photos that get uploaded for the whole world to see. We hope at the Oncoming Storm of Idaho that you feel welcomed and informed on things happening in your area. We want you to be prepared!
