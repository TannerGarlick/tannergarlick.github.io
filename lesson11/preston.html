Upcoming Events in Preston

Activites to do in Preston

By Tanner Garlick


If you or your family are thinking about moving to or visiting Preston, Idaho get ready for a great time! Preston is a lovely town that offers some great experiences for you and your loved ones. We have rafting, kayaking, and tubing rentals for as many people that are in your group. Enjoy the outdoors while staying cool. We also have a Riverdale Resort Hotel if you are looking for a getaway. It has recently been remodeled and is ready for you. We also have a bowling ally called Pop’n Pin Lanes. Looking to throw a birthday party? Well come on down to Pop’n Pin Lanes and have a great time! Preston also has a Golf and Country Club with a laid-back atmosphere where you will be able to relax and have a great time. Preston is waiting for you and your friends or family to arrive! Be sure to check the upcoming events above this article to stay in the loop.
