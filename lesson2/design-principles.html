Rule of Thirds

IGN

ign.com

IGN uses rule of thirds in the image for WandaVision season premiere. The left side is 1/3 of the image that is in color and the right side is 2/3 of the image that is in black and white.

Contrast

The Guardian

theguardian.com

The Guardian shows the use of contrast on their website by using bold red text at the begining of all their articles to attract the viewer's attention to the main point of each article.

Repetition

Ted

ted.com

Ted.com shows repetition by using the same red boxes and white text for the section topics. They also use the same fonts and font sizes for the titles of their videos.
