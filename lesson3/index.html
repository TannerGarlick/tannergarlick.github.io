Site Name

The site name is OCSofIdaho.com. OCS stands for Oncoming storm of Idaho.

Purpose Statement

Our purpose at The Oncoming Storm of Idaho is to not only share the weather with our community but to create an environment for the community to come together and learn about upcoming events, the weather, and locations near them. Our website is an oportunity for us to grow together and support eachother. We will have information about Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and the Storm Center. At The Oncoming storm we want you to be prepared!
